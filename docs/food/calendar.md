# Planting Calendar (Mediterranean Spain)

| Month | Indoor seeding (semillero) | Direct sow / transplant | Main harvest |
|---|---|---|---|
| Jan–Feb | Tomato, pepper, aubergine, celery, lettuce | Garlic, broad bean, pea, spinach | Kale, broccoli, endive |
| Mar–Apr | Courgette, cucumber, melon, watermelon | Lettuce, rocket, radish, onion | Peas, broad beans, spinach |
| May–Jun | — | Tomato (transplant), pepper, courgette, bean | Lettuce, radish, spring onion |
| Jul–Aug | Cabbage, broccoli, cauliflower (autumn) | Summer bean, beetroot, carrot | Tomato, pepper, cucumber, courgette |
| Sep–Oct | Lettuce, spinach, chard | Garlic, onion, spinach, autumn pea | Melon, watermelon, maize, pumpkin, potato |
| Nov–Dec | — | Broad bean, pea | Cabbage, broccoli, cauliflower, chard |

## Frost risk (heladas)

In Mediterranean Spain, frost risk is low below 600 m altitude but not zero.
- Last frost date: typically mid-February to mid-March
- First autumn frost: November–December
- Greenhouse provides ~4°C of frost protection for tender crops
